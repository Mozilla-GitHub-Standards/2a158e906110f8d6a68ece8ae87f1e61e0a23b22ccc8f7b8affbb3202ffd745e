Donate to Mozilla Accessibility

The Mozilla Accessibility community develops free, open source solutions for people with disabilities. The community works closely together on a variety of problems, such as basic web access for people with visual or physical impairments, fixing the accessibility problems of Web 2.0, and dreaming up the next great accessibility solutions. Many other projects also benefit from the work of the Mozilla Accessibility community.

If you would like to support Mozilla Accessibility initiatives, please consider making a donation. All donations can be made using a credit card, PayPal account or check. The Mozilla Foundation will use your donation to fund activities related to Mozilla Accessibility initiatives*.

Tax Exempt Status

The Mozilla Foundation is a California non-profit corporation exempt from federal income taxation under IRC 501(c)3. Donations are tax deductible for U.S. citizens. For donors outside of the United States, please consult with your tax advisor about whether your donation will be tax deductible.

* The Mozilla Foundation may use any directed donation for other purposes if the Board of Directors is unable to find suitable activities to fund for a project, if a project becomes inactive or otherwise becomes ineligible to receive funds.

** Your credit card or PayPal account will be automatically charged each month (read our Donation FAQ to find out how to cancel a monthly donation).
