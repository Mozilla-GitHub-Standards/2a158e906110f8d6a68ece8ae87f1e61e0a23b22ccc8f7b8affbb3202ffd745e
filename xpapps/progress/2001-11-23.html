Navigator Team Progress for the week ending // 2001

Highlights

  • Thoroughly enjoyed 4 day Thanksgiving weekend.
  • Bill Law made some interesting measurements comparing Mozilla/Netscape builds, and Modern/Classic themes.   Netscape is startup is slower than Mozilla's by up to  6.6%, and Modern's is slower than Classic's by up to 8.7%.
  • The Navigator team resolved 185 Bugzilla bugs in the last week, fixing 37 of these.  

Lowlights

  • Still a bit behind in triaging untargetted bugs, 316 in Bugzilla

              Bill Law (law):

 1. Completed startup "cost of chrome" measurements: 

    * links toolbar (bug 108616): ~3% (mozilla) ~.58% (ns) 

    * status/progress panel (bug 108617): ~.36% 

    * task-menu/taskbar (bug 108618): ~2.5% 

    * security icon (bug 108619): ~.18% < x < ~1.5% (lots 
of variation) 

    * throbber (bug 108620): ~.6% (again, with more variation here)
 

    * commercial vs. mozilla (bugscape bug 11051): 6.58% (classic) 
3.93%  (modern) (commercial is slower by those percentages) 

    * classic vs. modern (bugscape bug 11051): 8.67% (mozilla)
5.98%  (commercial) (modern is slower by those percentages)
