Navigator Team Progress for the week ending 10/26/ 2001

Highlights

  • Dave Hyatt et. al. continue to improve tabbed browser (try it, you'll like it!).
  • Joe Hewitt got outliner titletips implemented, along with XUL 1.0 work, and fixing a slew of  bugs for good measure.
  • Steve Morse completed implementation of Full Screen Mode.
  • Ben Goodger implemented Send Page/Link/Edit Page/Link.
  • Brian Ryner landed his tabbing rewrite, fixing a bunch o' bugs.
  • The Navigator team resolved 271 Bugzilla bugs  in the last week, fixing 80 of these.  

Lowlights

  • Too many bugs targetted for 0.9.6 ( 109 in bugzilla ).  
  • Still digging out from under our untargetted bugs, 435 in Bugzilla (269 confirmed).  We have triage team sessions scheduled every day this week to reduce these, and triage those bugs nominated for fixing in MachV and Mozilla1.0.
  • Matt Fisher finally ended his long gig at Netscape.  We'll miss him.
