Navigator Team Progress for the week ending 11/16/2001

Highlights

  • Navigator team is focussing on performance, with some good results:
    • Samir Gehani sped up search by eliminating some unecessary compiling of search results when the sidebar is closed.
    • Samire also improved startup time by removing the dependency of sidebarOverlay.xul on brand.dtd
    • Bill Law shortened startup by 3% by removing links toolbar.
    • Brian Ryner turned on his super-fast filepicker rewrite.
  • Joe Hewitt has the DOM Inspector ready to be put into mozilla builds.
  • The Navigator team resolved 196  bugs last week, 193 in Bugzilla , fixing 32 of these.

Lowlights

  • Once again behind in triaging our untargetted bugs, 259 in Bugzilla.  
  • Still have bugs targetted for mozilla1.0,   34  in Bugzilla
