Navigator Team Progress for the week ending 12/14/ 2001

Highlights

  • Ben Goodger landed his new Save As complete web page feature, giving Mozilla a real WYSIWYG save as feature!
  • Bradley Baetz landed SVG, though it is not turned on by default.
  • As part of theme simplification, Dave Hyatt designed a native theme rendering API (nsITheme), and partially implemented it on Win32.
  • Joe Hewitt  re-wrote about 80% of the modern, win classic, and mac classic themes, also to simplify.
  • Steve Morse freed up 50K of Wallet data, and knows how to free 50 more.
  • The Navigator team resolved 187 Bugzilla bugs in the last week, fixing 37 of these.
  • We now have an open req, and are actively recruiting to hire a software engineer.  Please see  Navigator team opening article for details.

Lowlights

  • Had to use Communicator 4.7 to to compose this because recent builds have been crashing left and right.  Bad dogfood!
  • Still digging out from under our untargeted bugs, 329 in Bugzilla.

Progress

- Bug 104158: use NS_LITERAL_STRING instead of *WithConversion("...")
- Bug 113228: Remove To{Lower,Upper}Case from ns{C}String.
