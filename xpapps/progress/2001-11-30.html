Navigator Team Progress for the week ending 11/30/2001

Highlights

  • Dave Hyatt has a new plan for significantly improving new window and startup by simplifying themes,  minimizing use of CSS and XBL, and possibly even  adding owner-draw.
  • Blake Ross landed bookmarksliner backend , which will speed up bookmark performance once he flips the switch.
  • Joe Hewitt turned on DOM Inspector and made the world a safer place for tooltips, details in his full report.
  • The Navigator team resolved 250 Bugzilla bugs in the last week, fixing 56 of these.

              Steve Morse (morse):

   

- Performance

Edit menu and context menu were taking considerable time to come up
depending on the content being viewed.  Succeeded in reducing that time
by half.  It's still too excessive and more work will need to be done
here.  But a factor of two speed-up is a good start.

Continued investigating time for page loading, specifically when forward
and back button is pressed.  Obtained some raw timing data, and also got
some quantify data.  However no progress was made in actually finding a
way to reduce this time.
