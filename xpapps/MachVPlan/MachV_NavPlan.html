Navigator Engineering Plan for MachV

 
Last Updated Thursday, November 08, 2001 14:26:40   

AOL/Netscape
Engineering Manager: Peter Trudelle
Table of Contents:

This document represents the results of a minimally sufficient amount of planning  aimed at achieving a MachV release of Navigator that satisfies marketing goals and engineering needs, within the given resource/time constraints.  It contains only publicly viewable plans, some Netscape/AOLTW proprietary information exists in separate documents behind the Netscape firewall.  

Performance: Overall area is owned by Paul Chen and David Hyatt.

Performance Resource allocation Plan

(1)  jag, pchen, hyatt  and bryner spend  20% of their time through 0.9.6, anaiyzing performance using timing dumps in JS and profiling in C++,  then addressing performance problems, enlisting assistance from anyone whose help is needed.
(2) XUL1.0 bugs should be prioritized based on perf gain.  hewitt should be the point man on XUL1.0 bugs, concentrating first on perf-enhancing XUL 1.0 bugs
