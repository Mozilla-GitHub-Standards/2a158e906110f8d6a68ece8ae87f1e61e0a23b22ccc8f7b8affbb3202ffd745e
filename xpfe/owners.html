XPToolkit Owners


A list of who owns what in XPToolkit-land. For questions related to individual widgets, please see our Widgets page.

Platform Owners

These people are in charge of platform-specific issues within the XPToolkit framework. They do not own the entire platform for all applications (eg, do not bother mcafee about Java on UNIX).

UNIX Chris Mcafee
