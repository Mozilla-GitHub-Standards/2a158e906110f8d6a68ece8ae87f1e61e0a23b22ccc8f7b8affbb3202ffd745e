UPDATE: xpfeViewer is dead! Long live xpAppViewer. Until xpAppViewer is more operational, use viewer to test html rendering.

The prototype application known as "xpfeViewer" has been scrapped. In its place lives a new application which is the foundation for the 5.0 product. All further development should be targeted at the appViewer, owned by the XPApps team. Our group never owned xpfeViewer (only the widgets and services it provided) and we will continue to develop those widgets/services in the new shell application.
