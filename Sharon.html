Sharon came to Netscape in late summer of '94 as a founding member of our QA department. She came for fun as much as anything else, and was quickly appreciated as a very valuable contributor to the team. Sharon was listed in various early Navigator about:authors pages as Chimp Wrangler, Plausible Deniability, and Octopus Wrangler.

Since then, Sharon remained in the middle of every Navigator release. While she was well respected for her opinions, clear thinking, and penetrating insight, she was also highly prized as a friend.

Last week, Sharon succumbed after a long fight with cancer. We at mozilla.org will miss her.
