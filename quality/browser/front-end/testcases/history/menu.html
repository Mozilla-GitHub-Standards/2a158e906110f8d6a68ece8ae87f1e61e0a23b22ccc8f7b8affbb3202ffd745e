Test Case Description for frame history

Purpose

This basic testcase is designed to verify that session history works for frames.

Initial Conditions

None

Steps/Description

  1. Click the link labeled "First" below.
  2. Click the link labeled "Second" below. Click "Back". Click "Forward".
  3. Click the link labeled "Third" below. Click "Back". Click "Forward".
  4. Click "Back" two times. Click "Forward" two times.

Expected Results

  1. In each case the bottommost frame should change to show you the number corresponding to where you are in the session history list(FIRST, SECOND or THIRD).
  2. Step(4) should walk you through the session history list in reverse then forward order.
