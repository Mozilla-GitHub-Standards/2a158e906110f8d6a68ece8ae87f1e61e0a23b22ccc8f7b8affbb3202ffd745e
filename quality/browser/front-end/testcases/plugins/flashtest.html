Test Case Description for testing Flash

Purpose

Determine that Macromedia flash plugin works properly

Initial Conditions

None

  2. Extensive list of flash test sites on poppy.macromedia.com
  3. Click here to visit a page with flash contents. Click on links on the page to view other pages with flash.
  4. Click here to visit a page with flash. Click on flash links on the page.
  5. Click here to visit the macromedia website. Click on links to load other shockwave pages
  6. Click here to visit the oddcast.com website.
  7. Click here to open the shockwave/flash gallery. Select the "archives" link to open a listing of flash enabled web pages
  8. Click here to view a list of flash cartoons
  9. Click here for viewing a flash enabled page
  10. Some more flash test pages:
    1. Page 1
    2. Page 2
    3. Page 3 (webtv testcase suite)
    4. Page 4 (flash tests)
  11. TestSuite from Macromedia(Internal URL): Click here

Expected Results

All flash movies should load and play properly.
