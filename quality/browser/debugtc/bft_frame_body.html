Frame Home Page

This window should be divided into two vertical frames, with no vertical scroll bars. The right vertical frame should have two horizontal panels. The upper panel is the "head" area and the lower right panel is the "body" area. This is the initial page that is displayed in the "body" area.

The set up for this page is this:


<FRAMESET COLS="20%,80%">

     <FRAME SRC="bft_frame_navpanel1.html" NAME="navpanel" NORESIZE SCROLLING=auto>
     <FRAMESET ROWS="25%,75%">
         <FRAME SRC="bft_frame_header.html" NAME="frm-bhd" NORESIZE SCROLLING=no>
         <FRAME SRC="bft_frame_body.html" NAME="frm-bdy-bdy" SCROLLING=auto>

     </FRAMESET>

</FRAMESET>
