IMAP Shared Folders Basic Functionality

Written by: Karen Huang

History:

 
Revision  Date Description Created/Updated By

  05/03/2002 Updated Karen Huang
     
IMAP Shared Folders : (Verify IMAP Shared Folders UI and Folders Privileges)
  1. Please Note
    1. Need to perform this feature on the Server with IMAP Shared Folder Enable.
    2. Please use EHS Messaging 6.0 Server (Server Name: tintin.mcom.com)
    3. Please use your tintin.mcom.com Server account (if you have) or select an available testing account from Mail Testing Accounts page and let Karen knows which account that you would like to perform the following Testing:

      4.  
  2. Verify IMAP Shared Folder UI
    1. Login to your tintin.mcom.com account.
    2. Select File|Subscribe to subscribe "ReadPrivilege", "RWPrivilege" and "RWMPrivilege" Folders under "Shared Folders/User/huang" of the Subscribe Dialog.
    3. From the 3-Pane window. Verify that you can view "huang" folders and messages under "Shared Folders/User/huang" in the 3-Pane window.
    4. Compare your Inbox and your other folders with "huang" account. Verify "huang" shared folders icons display differently. (Known bug that they are not displaying shared folder icons)
    5. Switch Themes to Classic and Modern. Verify UI display different "huang" shared folders icons for Classic and Modern. (Known bug that they are not displaying shared folder icons)

      6.  
  3. Verify IMAP Shared "Read Privilege" Folder
    1. Verify that you can view "ReadPrivilege" Folder messages from "huang" account.
    2. Verify that you CANNOT copy messages from your Inbox and your other folders to "huang" "ReadPrivilege" Folder.
    3. Verify that you CANNOT move messages from your Inbox and your other folders to "huang"  "ReadPrivilege" Folder.
    4. Verify that you CANNOT delete messages  from "huang"  "ReadPrivilege" Folder.

      5.  
  4. Verify IMAP Shared "Read and Write" Privilege Folder
    1. Verify that you can view "RWPrivilege" Folder messages from "huang" account.
    2. Verify that you can copy messages from your Inbox and your other folders to "huang" "RWPrivilege" Folder.
    3. Verify that you can move messages from your Inbox and your other folders to "huang"  "RWPrivilege" Folder.
    4. Verify that you can delete messages from "huang"  "RWPrivilege" Folder.

      5.  
  5. Verify IMAP Shared "Read, Write & Manage" Privilege Folder
    1. Verify that you can view "RWMPrivilege" Folder messages from "huang" account.
    2. Verify that you can copy messages from your Inbox and your other folders to "huang" "RWMPrivilege" Folder.
    3. Verify that you can move messages from your Inbox and your other folders to "huang"  "RWMPrivilege" Folder.
    4. Verify that you can delete messages from "huang"  "RWMPrivilege" Folder.
    5. Verify that you can create Subfolders for "huang"  "RWMPrivilege" Folder.
Please contact with Karen Huang if you have any questions.  Thanks.
