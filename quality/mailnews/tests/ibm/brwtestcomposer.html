Mozilla Composer can be invoked in the following ways.

Method 1:

  • Click on Start
  • Click on Programs
  • Click on Mozilla Composer suboption of Mozilla. ( Expecting in the Quint Build).


Method 2:

  • Bring up Mozilla browser.
  • Click on Composer option of Tasks menu.
Method 3:
  • Bring up Mozilla browser.
  • Click on File Menu.
  • Click on Edit New Blank Page sub option of  New.
Method 4:
  • Bring up Mozilla browser.
  • Click on composer (third) icon of the taskbar.
