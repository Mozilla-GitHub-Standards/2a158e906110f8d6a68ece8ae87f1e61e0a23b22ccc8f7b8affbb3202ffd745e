Blog Advertising

Many influential bloggers are blogging about their experience with Firebird and calling on their users to switch browsers. Personal experiences are more convincing than static, bland website advertisements. If you've got a blog, make it your goal to blog weekly or monthly about the benefits of Firebird. Blogging on a regular basis is much more effective than blogging once and having the advertisement get lost in your archives. In your blog, link to http://www.mozilla.org/products/firebird/, our brief sell/download page, or to http://www.mozilla.org/products/firebird/why/, our more elaborate "why you should switch" manifesto. For your convenience, we have provided some "talking points" below that you can copy and paste into a blog. Check your site's browser logs often and share your success stories!
