SeaMonkey BrownBag Training Series

The SeaMonkey BrownBag Training Series is a series of presentations created by and for Netscape engineers about various aspects of Netscape 6, which is based on Mozilla. The mozilla-related presentations are made available here publicly. These videos are recorded in the RealVideo format and can be viewed with RealPlayer 6.0 or greater.

For best results, save the files locally before viewing.
