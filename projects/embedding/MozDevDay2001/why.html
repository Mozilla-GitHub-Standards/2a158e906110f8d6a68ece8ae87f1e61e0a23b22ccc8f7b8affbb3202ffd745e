Why Mozilla [ << Previous | Contents | Next >> ]
  • Mozilla is open source.
  • Mozilla has strong browser technology and standards expertise.
Embedding motivation comes from...
  • users having more browser related feature needs than a single browser distributor can provide.
    • Features - such as MDI browser windows, and native UI.
    • Functionality - various, traditionally non-browser, applications need browser functionality.
    • Custom Devices - non-desktop devices often need to strip down the browser; "shrink to fit."
