Comments/Notes:
  • The images on this page were created from the PostScript jobs generated by Xprt's PS DDX. The jobs were converted to PNM via GhostScript, then scaled-down using pnmscale and finally converted to JPEG with cjpeg.
  • Note that the down-scaling and the JPEG format means that the quality is slightly reduced (e.g. "small" and "medium" image sizes are blurred), but that's the only way of getting very small files without relying on plugins or very large PNG files.
