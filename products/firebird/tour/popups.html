Popup Blocking

Ever visit a page and get bombarded by hordes of annoying ad windows? If you browse with Internet Explorer, Mozilla, Opera or Safari, you've undoubtedly had this happen to you at least once. Mozilla Firebird blocks popups right out of the box, so you're never bothered.



When Firebird encounters a popup, it places an icon in the status bar. If you want to see what's in the popup that was blocked, double click the icon and choose "Unblock Site" then visit the page that tried to open the popup again, and you'll see the popup window.
