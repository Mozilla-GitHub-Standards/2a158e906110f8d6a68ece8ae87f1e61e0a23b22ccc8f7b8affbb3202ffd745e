How It Works

The System

When you sign up for Firefox Affiliates, you will receive a user ID and some code samples to place on your website. How you promote Firefox is your own business. Your goal is to get people to click the Firefox link with your user ID. As people click these links, the Affiliates system keeps statistics. The user ID responsible for the most recent clickthrough a the time someone loads mozilla.org will have its associated website featured on the front page. We feel this is a fair method of cross promotion as it rewards high volume referral while nonetheless making it possible for lower volume referrers to be featured.

Promotion

As we said, how you promote is your own business, but we provide a few pointers in the form of default link code snippets, and a few buttons. Feel free to make your own. Personal recommendations generally work better than badges, so describe to your readers how Firefox has helped you. Highlight problems you had with your previous browser, and explain how they were remedied by using Firefox. Users respond better to software that targets their specific needs, so getting them to give Firefox a shot is a matter of identifying those needs and going after them.

Also note - this system works through email and IM too! E-mail your family and friends and get them to try Firefox. Just use the affiliate encoded URL instead of the mozilla.org link and your Affiliate account will be credited when they click through.

The Page

At present, we are linking the affiliates system through to the Firefox products page. We may offer additional options or change this link in the future but this should require no modifications on your part.

Hall of Fame

Affiliate referral statistics are tracked in the Hall of Fame page. This will also be linked from the front page, and the intent is to have this be sorted by number of referrers. Refer more people and improve your rank!
