Promoting Mozilla Thunderbird

Help spread the word about your favorite E-Mail client by taking advantage of our Thunderbird signature or promotional website buttons. When using the buttons, please bear in mind the Visual Identity Guidelines.

Signature Campaign

Use a signature in your outgoing e-mail which promotes Thunderbird such as: 
Reclaim Your Inbox!
http://www.mozilla.org/products/thunderbird/

Save our template signature on your machine. Go to Tools > Account Settings and for a particular account choose Attach this signature. Choose the template Thunderbird signature. Out going e-mail messages for this account will now include this signature.
