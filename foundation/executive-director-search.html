Executive Director Search

As of May 2007 the Mozilla Foundation is looking for an executive director to continue and expand upon the Foundation's activities to date. For more information see the detailed position specification.

For background information see the blog posts by Frank Hecker and Mitchell Baker. For updates on this process see the Executive Director Search Committee notes.

The public discussion forums for the job description, search process, and progress reports will be the mozilla.governance newsgroup (available via newsreaders or mailing list or via the browser). Mitchell may also post updates to her blog.
