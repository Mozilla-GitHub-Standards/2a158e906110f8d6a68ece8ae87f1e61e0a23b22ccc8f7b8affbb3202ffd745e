  • Physical Presence:

    Read all about it on the flyer and the FAQ.

  • Net Connectivity:

    We will be on the net, thanks to the generous help of the folks at Proxim, who are lending us the necessary hardware to get a wireless T1 link to the nightclub, and the folks at Hypnovista, who are providing the Internet connectivity at the other end.

  • MBone Video & Audio:

    One way to receive video and audio from the party is over the MBone (the IP Multicast backbone), if your ISP is connected to it.

    ICAST has graciously agreed to allow us to redistribute their Viewer & Guide for Windows 95 and NT to view the MBone video & audio. Here are the instructions.

    If you're running on another platform, or if you wish to interact (i.e. videoconference) with those of us at the party, you'll want to get the standard MBone tools. sdr, vic, and vat (or their equivalents) are the ones you care about.

    If you're already on the MBone and wish to reflect this to unicast destinations, feel free to do so. Send mail if you would like us to publish information here about any such service that you offer.

  • RealVideo Broadcast:

    Thanks to the fine folks at RealNetworks, we'll also be streaming out video from the party in RealVideo format. If you don't already have RealPlayer, you can get it here. When it gets rolling, the media should be available here.

  • Internet Relay Chat:

    Elliot Lee has been nice enough to provide an IRC server (irc.mozilla.org) where folks can chat about Mozilla online. We will probably have one or two terminals at the party connected up to this. If you haven't used IRC before, check out www.irchelp.org.

  • Thanks:

    In addition to the people and organizations listed above, a number of folks have generously lent us equipment and time to help make the technical side of this party a success. Many thanks!
