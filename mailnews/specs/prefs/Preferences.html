Summary 

This spec outlines the preferences for Mail/News and Address Book specifically. 

 

Design Details

Mail & Newsgroups preference item should be selected and expanded when a user enters Preferences from Mail or Newsgroups. Double clicking on an item with a twisty should select that item AND expand it's twisty.
