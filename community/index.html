Developer Forums

To foster open communication, mozilla.org hosts newsgroups, mailing lists and a chat server for contributing members.

mozdev.org hosts over 150 active projects related to Mozilla, including such popular projects as MultiZilla and XULMaker.

An active Japanese developer network, with its own Bugzilla .

Other Sites

mozilla.org technologies are utilized in a broad variety of software such as OEOne (Linux desktop environment), Nvu (Web authoring), and Xm (XML-based machine simulator).
