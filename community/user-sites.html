Mozilla English Community Sites

Outside of mozilla.org there are many personal and community sites dedicated to Mozilla, such as:

MozillaZine

An independent site, and the oldest around, devoted to news and opinions about Mozilla, brought to you by Chris Nelson.

Unofficial Mozilla Help Page

Gunnar's Mozilla site, containing FAQs, a general troubleshooting guide and several tutorials and how-tos that explain settings and security, and other features.

PluginDoc

Help site for installing and using helper applications (e.g. Flash and Java) and download managers.

MozillaNews

Another community site for news, editorial content, forums, BonsaiWatch, and other Mozilla-specific content.

Netscape DevEdge

Web developer site for Netscape 7 and Mozilla.

O'Reilly Network: Mozilla DevCenter

A good collection of articles for Mozilla developers and Web developers.

  • About.com Mozilla Resource Center - About.com's resource page for Mozilla, featuring many articles on using Mozilla, particularly tips on using Mozilla's mail client.
  • MozManual - A project for writing an introduction to Mozilla in print. The document is licensed under the Academic Free License.
  • Themes - A directory of Mozilla themes.
  • ExtensionRoom - A directory of Mozilla extensions (or add-ons).
  • gemal.dk - Henrik Gemal's home page, featuring information about Mozilla and a blog.
  • ZillaVilla: a House for Mozilla (Henrik Gemal) - A Mozilla site devoted to profiles and roaming issues.
  • UFAQ - the Netscape Unofficial FAQ - Probably the oldest active community site around, with answers to frequently asked questions about Mozilla and Netscape.
  • Techaholic.net Mozilla Help and Tips - Another tips & tricks page, with answers to over 50 frequently asked questions.
  • Mozilla Tips - With over 100 Mozilla tips, this site can make your life easier; brought to you by Robert Linwood and Jeffrey Linwood.
  • Netscape 7 Tips and Tricks (Holger Metzger) - An up-to-date site for Netscape 6/7, the siblings of Mozilla. Its extensive tips & tricks page has a lot of useful information for Mozilla users. The site hosts Netscape 7.1 Compact.
  • Netscape & Mozilla Solutions (Ramona) - A wealth of information on installing, using, and troubleshooting Mozilla and Netscape 7.
  • MozTips - Not to be confused with mozillaTips.com, Jayesh's site contains a wiki, documentation on profile, and tips, among other things. Some contents are still being moved from the old MozTips site.
  • Introduction to Thunderbird - A series of articles for beginners, by Kay Frode
  • Introduction to Firebird/Firefox - A series of articles for beginners, by Kay Frode
  • Chris Ilias's Mozilla Help site - Site of Chris Ilias, a Mozilla Champion
  • Ed Mullen's Mozilla page - Mozilla site from Ed Mullen.
  • Irwin's Information on Profiles - Information on working with profiles in Mozilla, Thunderbird, and Firefox. From Irwin Greenwald, a Mozilla Champion
  • MozillaZine Weblogs - A list of community members' blogs.
  • Blogzilla - A community blog about Mozilla.
  • The Burning Edge - Blog on the developments in nightly builds of Mozilla Firefox
  • Jesse Ruderman - Jesse Ruderman's site. It hosts the Burning Edge and many useful bookmarklets.
  • mpt - the Weblog of Matthew Thomas
  • European Mozilla - Organizer of the Mozilla Developers Meeting in Europe.
  • Mozilla UserGuide Project - A project for writing a user manual for Mozilla. Work is licensed under the Creative Commons License.
  • Scott Baker's Mozilla Starter Guide - A short introduction to some of Mozilla's coolest features.
  • Gérard Talbot's Firefox section - Information on Firefox
  • MozCafe
  • Warpzilla - If you are using Mozilla on OS/2, this is the place to go.
  • Mozilla Bookmarks - This site has a large number of Mozilla-related links (by category).
  • Automatic Mozilla Configuration - This page describes how to customize mozilla for a multi-user community.
