Contact Information

Due to the overwhelming popularity of Firefox, I receive a huge amount of e-mails every day. Therefore, I need to make a few things clear:

I am not a Firefox developer, and I am not a Firefox expert either. If you have end-user questions about Firefox itself (not this site), please visit the support forum. E-mails with questions about how to use Firefox are likely to be ignored.

That said, if you have a great tip about Firefox that you want to share with others, please let me know about it. Also, any suggestions, comments, or questions about this site is warmly welcomed, especially the positive feedback. Of course, if you just want to tell me how many people you've helped switching to Firefox, that's allright too!

If you have read everything above and still think that you should send me an e-mail, then go ahead. The email address is djst dot mozilla followed by the at-sign, and then gmail dot com. But as I said, don't bother sending me an e-mail if you're going to ask me questions about Firefox.
