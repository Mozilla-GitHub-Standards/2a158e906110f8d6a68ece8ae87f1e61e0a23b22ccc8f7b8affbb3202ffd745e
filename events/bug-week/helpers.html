Bug Week Helpers' Rota

Version 0.1, 2001-10-26

Things are complicated somewhat by a summer time change in the middle. Given that the majority of the helpers are on the US West Coast, I've kept the Pacific times constant, and moved the GMT ones.

I've tried to take everyone's preferences into account. A lot of you didn't say weekends were bad, but might have meant it. If you are down for a time that's inconvenient, let me know and I'll see what I can rearrange. If you can do more slots, that would be good to know too. Note that some people appear twice; only avincent appears more than twice.

Please, feel free to hang out in #bugweek and help out even when it's not your turn.

Mini-FAQ

What do I do?
  • Answer people's questions
  • If they have the capability to build, you might want to help them do that
  • There's a page of useful resources you might want to direct them to
  • Get them fixing bugs - bugs in your area of the product would be fine
  • Attempting to recruit new potential developers to work on your area of the project is absolutely permitted :-)
What happens if no-one turns up during my shift?
  • Then you can get on with some hacking of your own :-)
Do I need to change my nick?
  • Please - change to BW_<nick>. This is because I said this is what would happen (which may, in hindsight, have been a mistake, because I should have said to look for ops)
  • Ask someone to give you ops in #bugweek.
