Old Mozilla Milestone Plan

This page has been replaced by our roadmap and is kept here for historical purposes.

Disclaimers

All milestone dates are tentative and subject to change without notice. In other words, they are target dates, so don't take them so literally! Delays may happen.

This milestone plan is for mozilla.org's browser project, not Netscape's branded releases. Netscape's beta branch is only mentioned here to satisfy those who are interested in how Mozilla's progress may relate to Netscape 6's progress.

Take note that the milestone naming system has been changed! The milestone after M18 is Mozilla 0.6, NOT M19. Refer to the roadmap for more details.
