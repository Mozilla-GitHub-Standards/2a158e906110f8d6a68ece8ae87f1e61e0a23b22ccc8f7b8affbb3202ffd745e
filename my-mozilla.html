Other users of these feeds include my.userland.com, slashdot.org, and mozbot, our IRC robot which announces new headlines to the #mozilla channel on our IRC server at irc.mozilla.org.

Registered slashdot readers can customize it to display headlines from other sites including mozillaZine and the mozilla home page.
