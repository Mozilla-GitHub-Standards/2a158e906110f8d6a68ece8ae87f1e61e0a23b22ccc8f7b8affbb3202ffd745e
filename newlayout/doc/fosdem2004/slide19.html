Incremental Reflow

  • Mozilla starts to render a page as soons as enough content has arrived.
  • Content arriving later causes incremental reflows.
  • For example, images without a specified size will cause a reflow once they are loaded and the intrinsic size is known.
  • It is unpredictable how the content arrives and how the incremental reflows will be scheduled.
