Pompeii

Destroyed by the eruption of Mt. Vesuvius in 79 A.D., this magnificent Roman port city has recently been excavated and restored, in part, to its former glory. You can walk among the ruins, visiting such ruins as the baths, houses of noblemen, even bars and brothels!

Our package includes round-trip airfare from New York via Rome Fiumicino. Accomodation is at the Savoy Four Seasons, Napoli. You may also choose one of several tour packages to Salerno, Sorrento and the Amalfi Coast.

Venice

Dubbed 'La Serinissima' (the serene republic) by Italians, Venice is one of the most popular destinations in Europe. And for good reason! If you've never ridden in a gondola in the moonlight or sipped a cappuccino in St. Mark's square while people-watching, now is the time!

Our package includes round-trip airfare from New York via Milan. Accomodation is at the Jolly International Hotel in the main sector of Venice. You may also choose one of several tour packages to the island of Murano, Verona and Padua.

Prague

Since 1989's 'Velvet Revolution', Prague and the entire Czech Republic have become a major destination of travelers intent on seeing the former eastern bloc. While the communist years added a number of non-descript buildings to the Prague skyline, they also afforded the city a cocooned atmosphere which preserved much of its medieval charm.

Our package includes round-trip airfare from Washington National via Amsterdam Schipol. Accomodation is at the Holiday Inn Crowne Plaza in the historic 'Little Quarter' of Prague. You may also choose one of several tour packages to Prague Castle, Karelstejn and the Czech countryside.
