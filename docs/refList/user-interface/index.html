Designing the User Experience

Summary

    The purpose of this site is to serve as a resource for developers and designers who are interested in enhancing the user interface for Communicator. Included here are descriptions of design goals, design rationale, constraints and principles that have driven the Communicator design. Design specifications for future work, visual design specifications, and a Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) list are provided. We also provide a bibliography and related references to human-computer interaction.
