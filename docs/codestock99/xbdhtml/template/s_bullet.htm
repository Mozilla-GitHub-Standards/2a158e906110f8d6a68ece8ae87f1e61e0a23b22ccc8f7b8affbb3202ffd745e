Speaker Notes

Speaker notes elements are placed here, inside a <DIV ID="speakernotessection">.

  • They are hidden when the presentation is being used in front of an audience.
  • They can be shown when the speaker is studying the presentation.
  • Access them by:
    1. Editing style.jss and setting speakerNotesMode to true, OR moving style.jss into directory presstyl and moving the alternate style.jss from notestyl to the main directory.
    2. Opening the presentation using frameset document notes.htm, which will display a scrollbar in the center frame when needed to view long notes.
