Speaker Notes

Points to emphasize: Cacading Style Sheets bring the benefits of style sheets to the world of HTML. Now you can separate the formatting and content of HTML documents just as you do for word processing documents. This saves time and cuts costs, especially for long-term maintenance of large sites with long documents.
