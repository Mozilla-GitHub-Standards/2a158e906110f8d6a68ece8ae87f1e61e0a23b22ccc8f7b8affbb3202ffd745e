Title: Netscape Trademarks in Win32 Resources

Platform: Windows

Author: Rick Elliott
Last modified: 06 August 1998

History

18 June 1998: 1st revision
23 June 1998: 2nd revision. Added Ship's Wheel and Lighthouse notes.
06 Aug 1998: 3rd revision. Modified permissable component names. Added section on product naming.

Under the terms of the Universal Localization Program, you must remove or replace all trademarked Netscape terms, brand
names, icons, images, etc. These trademarks are listed on the page found at the following URL:

http://home.netscape.com/misc/trademarks.html#trademarks

This document identifies many, if not all, of the resources that contain trademarked information. While it is hoped this list is both definitive and correct, there may be other instances of trademarked strings, bitmaps, icons or other images in the product that are not listed. If you find an instance of what appears to be a trademarked item that is not listed in these pages, please let me know.

Summary
You may NOT use the following words, images or icons in your localized product (except to express Netscape Communications' copyright or trademarks):

  • The word "Netscape"
  • The word "Collabra"
  • The "N" logo
  • The "Lighthouse" image
  • The "Ship's Wheel" icon

You MAY use the following module names in your localized product:

  • Navigator
  • Communicator
  • Messenger
  • Composer
  • Conference
A Note on Naming Your Product
While you are free to name your product virtually anything you wish, you may not use a name that infringes on Netscape Communications Corporation's name. This means you may not use the phrases "net" or "scape" in your company or product name, nor may you create a company or product name that are clearly inspired by or parodies of "Netscape".

GIFs

(All trademarked GIFs are in RESDLL.DLL)
Name: "SPLASH_IMAGE" GIF
Example:
Resource ID(s): DATA:"SPLASH_IMAGE"
Instructions: You must remove or replace this with your own image.

This is a GIF89a-type file that is stored in the DATA resources of RESDLL.DLL. To remove it, simply edit RESDLL.DLL with a resource editor and delete the "SPLASH_IMAGE" DATA resource. You must also alter the <IMG SRC="about:logo" ALT="Netscape Logo"> tag in the about: page HTML. (This is contained in STRINGTABLE resources in RESDLL.DLL, starting at resource ID 60200.)

To replace this image with your own, follow these steps:

  • Open RESDLL.DLL with your resource editor
  • Again, using your resource editor, open the GIF image you want to replace the Netscape logo in another window.
  • Select the entire GIF image and copy it to the clipboard.
  • Switch to the window containing RESDLL.DLL, locate the DATA section and open the "SPLASH_IMAGE" resource.
  • Select the entire "SPLASH_IMAGE" resource, then paste the new image over it.
  • Be sure the resource is null terminated (ends with 00)
  • If your image size is different from the original Netscape one, you might also want to alter the HEIGHT and WIDTH elements of <IMG SRC="about:logo"> tag in the about: page HTML.
  • Save the edited copy of RESDLL.DLL.
(These instructions were derived by using Microsoft MSDEV. If you use a different resource editor, adjust these instructions accordingly.)
